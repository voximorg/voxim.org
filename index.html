The Voxim Project

Voxim is a new gaming initiative by Holden Green and The Green Empower Organization. Voxim stands for voxel simulation.

Voxim is a branch of the The Green Empower Organization

Voxim shall be a modernized and improved alternative to Minecraft that is released as free software.

Voxim will be faster and more engineered than Minecraft. Both technically and artisticly.

Many of the speed increases gained by engineering and design choices will be used to improve the fidelity of the game.

Voxim will target mid to high range hardwareof the current era in the expectation that lower end hardware will catch up by the time that Voxim gains wider adoption.
